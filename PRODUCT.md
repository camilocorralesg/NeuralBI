# Product

## Register
brand

## Users
Modern enterprises, tech leaders, and stakeholders looking for an AI-native consulting blueprint. Users expect a premium, high-performance experience.

## Product Purpose
Introduce NeuralBI as a modern alternative to traditional IT consulting. Showcase its arsenal (PowerBI, PowerApps, Copilot, Azure) and industry verticals (Fintech, Retail, etc).

## Brand Personality
Premium, ultra-modern, confident, futuristic. "Mainly dark premium style".

## Anti-references
Bland SaaS, traditional IT consulting (Accenture, Deloitte), cheap generic templates. Avoid light/cream/sand backgrounds. Avoid standard Bootstrap/Tailwind defaults.

## Design Principles
1. Premium tactile glassmorphism.
2. Seamless gradients and ambient glows.
3. High-performance snappy motion.
4. "Dark premium style" - default to deep, rich dark themes with vibrant neon/lime accents.
5. "Keep our font stack" - strictly adhere to `--font-sans`, `--font-ui`, `--font-mono`, `--font-display`.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Maintain minimum contrast ratios for readable text, even in dark mode. Ensure interactive elements have distinct hover and focus states.
